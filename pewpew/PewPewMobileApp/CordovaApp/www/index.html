Welcome to the Pew Pew game.

The object of the game is to shoot as many enemies as you can without being blown up yourself. You get three lives per play, but every time you hit an enemy the next one comes at you a bit faster so watch out!

This game is an experiment in making games using HTML5, Bootstrap and a couple of other cool things. I know it's a bit rough, but hey you don't need much to have fun, right?

Enjoy :)
